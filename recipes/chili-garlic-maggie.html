CHILLI GARLIC MAGGIE

Description

Chilli Garlic Maggie is a fiery and flavorful take on classic instant noodles. With a zesty blend of red chillies and garlic, these noodles are cooked to perfection and tossed in a spicy chili garlic sauce. The result is a deliciously bold and aromatic dish that adds an exciting twist to your noodle experience, perfect for spice enthusiasts seeking a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggie Masala
  • 40g Garlic Butter
  • 3-4 Garlic Cloves
  • Half Chopped Small Onion
  • 2 spoon Soya Sauce
  • 2 1/2 spoon Red Chilli Sauce
  • Vegetables of your choice

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles in a pot, excluding the masala, and drain the water.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic and onions.
  3. Add soya sauce and red chilli sauce to the pan.
  4. Mix in the Maggie masala thoroughly.
  5. Introduce your preferred vegetables and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Combine the boiled noodles with the flavorful mixture in the pan, ensuring an even mix.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious dish.
